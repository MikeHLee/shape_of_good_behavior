### Topological Social Choice

29. **Arrow, K.J.** (1951). *Social Choice and Individual Values*. Wiley.

31. **Baryshnikov, Y.** (1993). "Unifying Impossibility Theorems: A Topological Approach." *Advances in Applied Mathematics*, 14(4), 404-415.
    - Unifies Arrow and Chichilnisky via sphere non-contractibility

32. **Anonymous** (2024). "Localizing Preference Aggregation Conflicts: A Graph-Theoretic Approach Using Sheaves." *arXiv:2512.02416*.

**Our contribution**: First to apply sheaf cohomology to RLHF reward modeling for consistency detection and safety guarantees.
